## Challenges and Solutions

- Funding gaps: Access to our investor network
- Talent acquisition: Partnerships with universities and recruiters
- Market access: Distribution through our portfolio channels
- Technology limitations: R&D collaborations and shared infrastructure

<Callout kind="tip">Early engagement with our team significantly improves startup outcomes.</Callout>
